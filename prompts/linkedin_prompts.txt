# LinkedIn Prompt Templates

## Professional Insight
Create a LinkedIn post sharing a professional insight about {topic}.
Structure:
1. Personal experience or observation
2. Key insight or learning
3. Relevant statistics or data
4. Call to action (share your thoughts, connect)
5. Professional tone throughout

## Thought Leadership
Write a thought leadership post about {topic}.
Include:
1. Strong opening hook
2. Unique perspective or contrarian take
3. 3-5 key points
4. Industry context
5. Ask for comments/discussion

## Career Advice
Create a LinkedIn post offering career advice about {topic}.
Format:
1. Relatable problem or situation
2. 3 actionable tips
3. Personal example or story
4. Encouragement/motivation
5. Invite conversation

## Company Culture
Write a post about company culture/achievement related to {topic}.
Should include:
1. What the company did
2. Impact and results
3. Team appreciation
4. Values aligned with action
5. Pride in accomplishment

## Educational Content
Create an educational LinkedIn post about {topic}.
Provide:
1. Clear topic introduction
2. 3-5 learning points
3. Actionable takeaways
4. Resource link if available
5. Encourage sharing and learning
