## 📊 Key Metrics Reference

### Optimization Scores (Omneky)
- **Hook Strength** - Attention grabbing (0-100)
- **Emotional Appeal** - Power words (0-100)
- **Clarity** - Message simplicity (0-100)
- **CTA Effectiveness** - Call-to-action strength (0-100)
- **Platform Fit** - Format compliance (0-100)

### Performance Predictions (Madgicx)
- **Est. CTR** - Click-through rate (%)
- **Est. CPC** - Cost per click ($)
- **Quality Score** - Content quality (0-100)
- **Audience Alignment** - Audience fit (0-100%)
- **Scaling Status** - Readiness to scale

**Learn more:** [OMNEKY_MADGICX_GUIDE.md](OMNEKY_MADGICX_GUIDE.md#performance-metrics)
